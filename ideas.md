# Feature Ideas

## Achivements
- **Most games played**
- **Stalest tier list**

## Roles
- Prefixes for games owned

## Social / Group

- **Game night planner** — pick a date, vote on games from the collection, auto-filter by player count of RSVPs
- **Play log** — record when you actually played a game, who won, track plays over time
- **Hot takes wall** — short anonymous or named takes ("Catan is carried by nostalgia") that others can agree/disagree with
- **Shame board** — games owned the longest with zero plays

## Collection

- **"Convince me" queue** — nominate a game you want the group to try, others upvote/downvote
- **Expansion tracking** — link owned expansions to base games
- **Lending tracker** — who has whose game right now
- **Dust index** — sort by time since last tier list update or play (if play logging exists)

## Discovery

- **"If you like X"** — recommend similar games from the collection based on shared mechanics/categories from BGG data
- **Blind taste test** — show two random game descriptions (no names/images), pick which sounds better, reveal results
- **BGG deep cuts** — surface highly-rated BGG games matching the group's preferred mechanics/weight that nobody has added yet

## Competitive / Fun

- **Prediction market** — before someone tries a new game, everyone predicts what tier they'll place it
- **Tier list bingo** — generate bingo cards from group tier data ("someone rates Azul below C", "unanimous S-tier")
- **Bounties** — challenges like "play a game over 3.5 weight" or "rate 5 new party games this month"
- **Seasonal awards** — quarterly superlatives voted on by the group

## Stats

- **Tier list drift** — track how ratings change over time (did a game fall from grace?)
- **Taste clusters** — visualize who has similar taste beyond just the alignment table
- **Weight vs enjoyment scatter plot** — are heavier games actually rated higher?
